Welcom to Food Trip

On this part, shows the different food that every Hinunangons are proud of.

Desserts

Buko Pie

The provinve of Hinunangan are very rich in coconut tree everywhere you look there are so many tress of coconut, this dessert are always sell in terminal because the word "Hinunangan" came from the word "Hununangan" which means stop over. That's if you go in this province and went to their terminal there are many people sell buko pie.

Ginusang

Ginusang is one of the common and tastier dessert that be marketable, it made by corn and coconot milk or cold "Latik". This dessert is easier than buko pie because you only need the grind corn, margarine, sugar, and coconot milk and cook it for about 20 minutes and there you go, your ginusang has already cook

Dish

Pijako

Pijako is one of the very simple dish that can tourist buy if they taste, because of the sourness and the spice that it have. Tourist can eat this while they were drinking.

Sinugba

Sinugba is a common dish but it always there when their are celebrations like fiestas and birthday. This dish are very simple it always be remember that sinugba words are to grill, that is why it has smokey smell and take note it become more deliciuos if you deep in vinegar and soy souce with so much red onion.

Lechon

This lechon is always serve in celebrations and the 40 days of death
